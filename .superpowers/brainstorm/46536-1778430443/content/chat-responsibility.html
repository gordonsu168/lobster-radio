How does the Chat interact with the DJ and Music?

When you chat with the new Agent, how should it optimize recommendations and narration?

A

Implicit Profiling (Background Updates)

The Chat Agent acts as a friendly listener. It secretly extracts your preferences/mood from the conversation and saves them to the Memory System, which the DJ uses to play better music and adjust its script naturally.

B

Direct Command (The Chat is the DJ)

You are chatting directly with the DJ. If you say "Play something upbeat", the Chat Agent immediately skips the track, queues upbeat music, and replies in character.

C

Hybrid (Producer + DJ)

You chat with a "Producer" agent to discuss what you want. The Producer updates settings, and the DJ (NarratorAgent) continues the show based on the new settings, mentioning your requests on air.
