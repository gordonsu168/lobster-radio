How should we arrange the Right Sidebar?

Currently, the History panel and Track Queue take up space on the right. How should the Chat fit in?

A

Tabs (Chat / Queue / History)

The right sidebar becomes a tabbed container. You switch between Chat, Track Queue, and History. Clean and organized.

B

Split View (Chat Top, Queue Bottom)

The Chat is always visible at the top of the sidebar, with a smaller Track Queue/History underneath it. Good for multi-tasking.

C

Chat Replaces History

History and Queue move somewhere else or are simplified, dedicating the entire right column to the Chat experience.
